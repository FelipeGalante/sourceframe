# Product Templates

This hub collects copyable, generic product-document templates. Each page is intentionally written so you can duplicate it, rename it, and replace the sample content with your own product details.

## Templates

- [PRD Template](./prd.md)
- [Research Template](./research.md)
- [Roadmap Template](./roadmap.md)
- [Feature Matrix Template](./feature-matrix.md)
- [Persona Template](./personas.md)
- [Success Metrics Template](./success-metrics.md)
- [Release Plan Template](./release-plan.md)

## Copy flow

1. Duplicate the template page you want to reuse.
2. Rename the file and adjust the `title`, `summary`, and `product:` block.
3. Replace the sample content with your own product details.
4. Keep the template headings if you want the page to stay readable while you iterate.
